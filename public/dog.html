10 common dog behaviours
SN Behaviour Problems Causes Solution
1 Excessive Barking Boredom lack of mental stimulation little dog training,teaching them short and fun tricks
2 chewing and destructive behaviour dogs natural instinct to explore environment Plenty of exercise and mental stimulation
3 Digging Boredom, something buried beneath the death eliminate the source of digging aand provide alternatives like walks games etc
4 Jumping on people This is a dog natural behaviour when they are excited or trying to get attention Teaching them the 'sit' command and reward her with treats when she complies
5 Separation and anxiety Mostly when the owner is not around, when they are left alone Provide interactive toys,take them to a dog trainer
6 Niping or play biting Over excitement Plenty of exercise,teach them to stay calm
7 Chasing other dogs Its a sign of predatory or hunting behaviour Teaching him to respond to commands like"come","leave it","stop"
8 Begging Begging is a bad habit, dogs beg because they love food Avoid feeding him from the table or giving him other peoples food
9 Inappropriate elimination An act to unwelcome guest, can also be caused by anxiety,fear Discuss their behaviours with the vetenarian
10 Aggression Fear,dominance,pain,frustration Taking the dog for walk regularly
